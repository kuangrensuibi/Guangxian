Guangxian Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

Thank you for using "Guangxian" (the "App"). We take your privacy very seriously. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

In one sentence: all processing happens locally on your device. The App does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

The App has no accounts, requires no registration, and transmits no data over the network. We therefore do not collect your name, phone number, location, device identifiers, or any other personal information.

2. Photo Library (Photos / Videos) Access

To enable video editing and color grading, the App accesses your photo library only when you actively choose to:

  • read the videos/images you select and import them into the App for local editing;
  • save the finished video back to your library when you tap Export.

This media is processed only locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server. We never read content you have not actively selected.

3. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is inaccessible to us:

  • your editing drafts and project files;
  • the first-launch timestamp used to compute the remaining time of the "7-day free trial" (anonymous time data stored in the system Keychain).

4. Purchases & Payment

The "Pro" version is a one-time in-app purchase, with payment handled by the Apple App Store. We do not access or store your payment information (card numbers, etc.). Purchase receipts are verified locally on-device by Apple's StoreKit.

5. Third Parties

The App integrates no third-party SDKs, contains no ads, and includes no analytics/statistics tools. We do not share data with any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

7. Policy Updates

If this policy changes, we will post the latest version on this page and update the effective date.
