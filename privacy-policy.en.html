光显 Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

Thank you for using “光显” (the “App”). We take your privacy very seriously. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

In one sentence: all processing happens locally on your device. The App does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

The App has no accounts, requires no registration, and transmits no data over the network. Therefore we do not collect any personal information such as your name, phone number, location, or device identifiers.

2. Photo Library (Photos / Videos) Permission

To enable video editing and color grading, the App accesses your photo library only when you actively choose to, in order to:

  • Read the videos/images you select and import them into the App for local editing;
  • Save the finished video back to your photo library when you tap Export.

This media is processed only locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server. We do not read anything you have not actively selected.

3. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is not accessible to us:

  • Your editing drafts and project files;
  • The first-launch timestamp used to calculate the remaining time of the “7-day free trial” (anonymous time data, stored in the system Keychain).

4. Purchases and Payment

The App’s “Pro” version is a one-time in-app purchase, with payment processed by the Apple App Store. We neither access nor store your payment information (card numbers, etc.). Purchase receipts are validated locally on the device by Apple’s StoreKit.

5. Third Parties

The App integrates no third-party SDKs, contains no ads, and includes no analytics/tracking tools. It does not share data with any third party.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

7. Policy Updates

If this policy is updated, we will post the latest version on this page and update the effective date.
